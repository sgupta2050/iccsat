About the Conference

The 1st International Conference on Computing and Sustainable Automation Technology (ICCSAT-2027) is a global event that brings together experts, researchers, and practitioners to explore the intersection of computing, automation, and sustainability.

The conference showcases the latest advancements in research and development in computation and automation technologies that contribute to sustainable practices across various industries. It fosters global collaboration between researchers, professionals, and practitioners to exchange ideas, innovations, and methodologies.

Conference Objectives

  • Showcase Innovation: Highlight implementation of computational and automation technologies that have led to more sustainable operations in sectors like Manufacturing, Healthcare and Medicine, Construction, Energy, Agriculture, and Transportation.
  • Encourage Interdisciplinary Discussions: Integrate various fields such as computer engineering, electrical & electronics engineering, environmental sciences, and social sciences to promote sustainable automation technologies.
  • Address Global Challenges: Highlight the role of automation in tackling climate change, resource management, and energy efficiency.
  • Explore Future Technologies: Examine the future of automation technologies, including AI, robotics, IoT, and machine learning, and their sustainable applications.

About Jaipur - The Pink City

Rajasthan's beautiful Pink City Jaipur was the stronghold of a clan of rulers whose three hill forts and series of palaces in the city are important attractions. Known as the Pink City because of the color of the stone used exclusively in the walled city, Jaipur's bazaars sell embroidered leather shoes, blue pottery, tie and dye scarves and other exotic wares.

Founded in AD 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is steeped in history and culture. Here the past comes alive in magnificent forts and palaces where once lived the maharajas. The bustling bazaars of Jaipur, famous for Rajasthani jewelry, fabric, and shoes, possess a timeless quality and are surely a treasure-trove for shoppers.

This fascinating city with its romantic charm takes you to an epoch of royalty and tradition. Jaipur has been laid according to the conventional nine-grid pattern that astrologers believe to be lucky and which has been recommended in ancient Indian treatise on architecture. Today, these walls may be more difficult to spot since the city has grown far beyond its original plan, but they are still there, proof that though Jaipur saw no great siege, it was more than adequately prepared for it.

About Poornima College of Engineering

Poornima College of Engineering (PCE) was established in the year 2000. PCE is the flagship institution of the Poornima Group of Colleges. There are seven B.Tech. courses:

  • Computer Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering
  • Information Technology
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering

In a span of twenty-four years, PCE has added many feathers in its cap and has emerged as the top-notch destination for technical education in India.
